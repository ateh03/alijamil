About Ali

(Ali Jamil) علی جمیل

Hi, I’m Ali Jamil and welcome to my GLD ePortfolio! I am a current senior at the University of South Carolina majoring in Computer Information Systems and minoring in Business Information Management, and will be graduating with Leadership Distinction in Professional and Civic Engagement. I am Black and Iranian…

As the time between now and my graduation only continues to wane, I am both wildly excited and terribly sad. These four years diving headfirst into adulthood and technology have shaped who I am, and while I am hesitant to leave USC, all good things must come to an end. Thanks for taking the time to peruse my ePortfolio. I hope the various whims, dreams and epiphanies I’ve conceived during my four collegiate years leave you with a sense of inspiration going forward.

-Ali
